THANDOORI

Tandoori chicken is a chicken dish prepared by roasting chicken marinated in yogurt and spices in a tandoor, a cylindrical clay oven.

CHICKEN LOLLOPOP

Chicken lollipop is, essentially a frenched chicken winglet,
wherein the meat is cut loose from the bone end and pushed down creating a lollipop appearance.

CHICKEN WINGS

Summer just isn't the same without wings. Regardless of how they're cooked or the sauce they're tossed with, wings always hit the spot, particularly when paired with a A+ cocktails and dipping sauces.

CHICKEN BIRYANI

Chicken Biryani is a savory chicken and rice dish that includes layers of chicken, rice, and aromatics that are steamed together.
